Capri

Capri is a stunning island located in the Bay of Naples in Italy. It is known for its natural beauty, crystal-clear waters, and
luxurious lifestyle. Visitors can explore the beautiful Blue Grotto, take a boat tour around the island, and enjoy the charming town
of Capri with its boutique shops and delicious Italian cuisine.
