Ravello

Ravello is a charming hilltop town on the Amalfi Coast of Italy. It is known for its stunning villas, beautiful gardens, and panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea. Visitors can explore the historic center, visit Villa Cimbrone and Villa Rufolo, and enjoy the tranquil atmosphere of this picturesque town.
