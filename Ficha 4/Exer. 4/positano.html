Positano

Positano is a beautiful coastal town on the Amalfi Coast of Italy. It is known for its colorful buildings, stunning cliffs, and
crystal-clear waters. The town is perched on the cliffs and offers breathtaking views of the Mediterranean Sea. Visitors can
enjoy the picturesque beaches, explore the charming streets, and savor delicious Italian cuisine.
