Sorrento

Sorrento is a charming coastal town located on the Amalfi Coast in Italy. It offers breathtaking views of the Bay of Naples and Mount Vesuvius. Visitors can explore the historic center, enjoy the local lemon-flavored cuisine, and take day trips to nearby attractions like Pompeii and the Isle of Capri.
