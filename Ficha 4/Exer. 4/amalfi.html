Amalfi

Amalfi is a picturesque coastal town on the Amalfi Coast of Italy. It is known for its stunning cathedral, historic center, and
beautiful beaches. Visitors can explore the charming streets, enjoy traditional Italian cuisine, and take in the breathtaking
views of the Mediterranean Sea.
