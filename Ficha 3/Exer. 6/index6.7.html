5 MYTHS ABOUT BOOKING A FLIGHT THAT YOU NEED
TO IGNORE

Last Updated: 8/28/23 | August 28th, 2023

Let’s talk about cheap flights. We all know airlines are out to screw us over
— and no one wants to be the person who gets stuck paying the highest
fare. That’s why we spend hours upon hours researching articles on airfare,
trying to game the system like we’re attempting to outsmart a used car
salesman.

I’ve written about finding a cheap flight before — and even my process
for booking a flight — but today I want to talk about some persistent and
inaccurate myths about booking a flight that have stuck around through
simple inertia and lazy journalism.
